Tuition

Create Profile

First You need to Create a Tuition Profile for getting Notifications while someone makes a post in need of a teacher. So, for getting Notifications you have to create a tuition profile with the actual details needed. Your provided information will match with the tuition posts which is for in search of a teacher.

Get Notifications

The information provided in teachers tuitionprofile will match with the requirements of a post of Tuition that is in search of a teacher.Then a Notifications will posted to the teachers profile.

Post Tuition

If you need a teacher then please Create a post to find the best teacher for you. You have to make your post with proper requirements and if any teachers profile information matches with your requirements then that teacher will be notify with your post link and by visiting your post he can apply for the tuition with his/her contact information.Then you can see their profile for choosing the best one. People also can like and comment on your post.

New to DesireBd?

If you want to find a tuition from previously made post then simply View post and filter post related to your needs.

Add Education Details

If you are a teacher then Add education details to give information about your education for creator of tuition post. Thus they can hire you for themselves.





Metrimony

Metrimony Profile

Create a metrimony profile for giving information about yourself for someone who is waiting for you. You have to provide some basic information so that someone can know about yourself and getting interest about you.

Metrimony Expectation

Need to find matches for you? Then Make an expectation profile with your requirements. Then when you click find matches program will matches your expectation profile with some other opposite genders information.

Metrimony Find Best match:

Then you can see the List of best matches and by clicking the persons name you can see their profile and contact information. Then what you will is depends on all about you..
